Born and raised in Port Elizabeth, Simbarashe discovered his passion for fitness during his own transformation journey. What started as a personal mission to build strength and discipline quickly became a calling to help others do the same.

With a philosophy rooted in mental resilience as much as physical gains, Young Lion Fitness was born. Today he coaches clients online and in‑person, proving that the right mindset can turn any body into a fortress.
